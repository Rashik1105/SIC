The SIC Journey

2020

The Beginning

Founded by a team of marketers and tech entrepreneurs who saw the gap between content creators and businesses looking to partner with them.

2021

Platform Launch

SIC platform officially launched with core features for matching influencers with relevant businesses and tracking campaign performance.

2022

Growth & Expansion

Expanded our team and added advanced analytics features to help creators understand their audience and optimize content strategy.

Introduced monetization tools and expanded resources to help creators build sustainable businesses beyond one-off brand deals.

2024

2025

The Future

Continuing our mission to innovate and empower creators with cutting-edge tools and meaningful business connections.
